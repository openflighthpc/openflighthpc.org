What is the hub?

The OpenFlightHPC Hub is a CentOS 7 image loaded with the OpenFlightHPC tools and some additional content to get a cluster up and running.

The hub is packaged with flight-architect, flight-cloud and flight-metal. Allowing for control over configuration, deployment and management of a cluster. Additionally, the system is configured to share the content files and scripts out-of-the-box, allowing for a streamlined cluster deployment process.

Launch your own hub today by following the hub deployment guide.
