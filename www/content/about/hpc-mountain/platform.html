--- heading: Platform ---

This is the second level of the HPC mountain. Platform consists of the deployment and management of resources on the cluster platform.

The platform stage largely involves configuring the base level of resources, namely:

  • Deploying Resources - Building the hardware or configuring the virtual/cloud resources to be as desired.
  • Power Management - Remotely checking and changing the power status of resources.

Watch this space for the release of tools and documentation for this stage of the HPC Mountain
